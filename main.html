Projects

Comparative Analysis of PPO and GRPO for Solving Wordle

Trained PPO and GRPO agents to solve Wordle across 2,309 official answer words, then compared them on win rate, guess efficiency, constraint reduction, policy entropy, and generalization. PPO converged to a stable, high-performing policy; GRPO looked competitive early on, then collapsed under extended training, exposing a sharp gap between information-seeking and actually acting on information.

89.6% PPO win rate5.8% GRPO win ratePPOGRPORL

Predicting Failure in Offline Reinforcement Learning from Dataset Quality Ongoing

Investigating how the quality and coverage of a fixed offline dataset drives the failure modes of conservative offline RL algorithms (CQL, IQL) on the D4RL benchmark, through systematic coverage-reduction, quality-degradation, and noise-injection studies, with the goal of predicting failure before training even starts. Currently working through a CQL collapse under degraded data quality.
